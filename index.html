1. Brand Overview

Angor is committed to providing a secure, transparent, and decentralized crowdfunding platform, allowing investors to manage their funds safely and founders to access a trustless funding solution.

2. 🚀 Brand Mission & Values

Mission

Angor's mission is to revolutionize crowdfunding through decentralized blockchain technology, empowering creators and investors with transparent, trustless funding solutions that eliminate intermediaries and reduce barriers to innovation.

Vision

We envision a world where funding creative and innovative projects is accessible to all, free from traditional financial gatekeepers, and built on the principles of transparency, security, and community ownership.

Core Values

Transparency

We believe in complete openness in all operations, enabled by blockchain technology.

Security

We prioritize the safety of user funds and data through robust technological solutions.

Decentralization

We are committed to reducing central points of control and empowering our community.

Innovation

We continuously explore new technological solutions to improve the crowdfunding experience.

Accessibility

We strive to make our platform usable by everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Community

We foster connections between creators and backers to build lasting relationships.

7. 📷 Photography Style

The photography style for Angor should reflect our brand values of transparency, innovation, and accessibility while maintaining a professional and clean aesthetic.

Style Guidelines

  • Use high-quality images with clear, sharp focus and good lighting
  • Prefer images with a modern, tech-oriented feel that relates to blockchain, finance, or innovation
  • Choose images with a balanced color palette that complements our brand colors
  • When featuring people, ensure diversity and authentic representations
  • Avoid overly processed, artificial-looking images or cliché stock photos

8. 🗣️ Brand Voice & Tone

The Angor brand voice helps establish a consistent personality across all communications, reflecting our values and connecting with our audience.

Voice Characteristics

Knowledgeable

We communicate with expertise and authority on blockchain technology without being condescending.

Accessible

We explain complex concepts in simple terms, avoiding unnecessary jargon.

Trustworthy

We are straightforward and honest, focusing on facts and transparency.

Forward-thinking

We express excitement about innovation and the future possibilities of technology.

Tone Guidelines

Our tone can adjust based on the context while maintaining our core voice:

Marketing & Website

Confident and inspiring, highlighting benefits and possibilities

Example: "Angor empowers creators with trustless funding solutions, removing barriers and unlocking new possibilities."

Technical Documentation

Clear and precise, focusing on accuracy and helpful guidance

Example: "Connect your wallet by clicking the 'Connect' button in the top-right corner and selecting your preferred wallet provider."

Community Engagement

Conversational and responsive, showing genuine interest in user feedback

Example: "Thanks for sharing your experience! We're looking into this issue and will update everyone once we have a solution."

9. 🎯 Visual Guidelines

Guidelines for maintaining consistency in logo, color, and typography usage.

  • Maintain clear space around the logo to ensure it stands out.
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or alter the proportions of the logo.
  • Use the appropriate dark or light version depending on the background color.
  • Use Very Dark Teal as the main background or for large sections.
  • Use Dark Cyan for highlights, buttons, and interactive elements.
  • Use Light Steel Green for text or background highlights.
  • Use Inter for headings and body text to ensure consistency.
  • Minimum font size for web: 16px. Minimum size for print: 12pt.

12. ♿ Accessibility Guidelines

Angor is committed to creating products and communications that are accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Color Contrast

All text must have sufficient contrast with its background to ensure readability.

  • Normal text must have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 against its background
  • Large text (18pt or 14pt bold) must have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1
  • UI components and graphical objects must have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1

Good contrast: White text on very dark teal

Good contrast: Very dark teal text on white

Poor contrast: White text on light steel green

Text Alternatives

Provide text alternatives for all non-text content:

  • All images must include descriptive alt text
  • Videos should include captions and audio descriptions when necessary
  • Icons should have descriptive labels or aria-labels

Keyboard Navigation

All functionality must be available via keyboard:

  • Ensure all interactive elements can be accessed and operated with keyboard only
  • Maintain logical tab order that follows the visual layout
  • Provide visible focus indicators for all interactive elements

13. 📄 Brand Applications

Website

Ensure consistency in applying logos, colors, and typography across all pages. Navigation should be clear and user-friendly, following the brand's tone.

Social Media

Use the square version of the logo for profile pictures and avatars. Maintain the brand color palette and typography in promotional posts.

Marketing Materials

Apply logos, fonts, and colors as specified in all brochures, presentations, and advertisements. Always use vector logos for print materials to ensure clarity.

14. 📜 Legal and Compliance

The Angor logo and brand elements must not be altered in any way. Ensure compliance with Angor's intellectual property and trademark guidelines.
